Tentang Saya

Real name: Wan Aiman bin Wan Aaqashah — a young independent singer and songwriter from Malaysia. I was born on 5 October 2010 and will be turning 15 soon. Despite my age, music has already become a deep passion and purpose in my life. As Aiman Valoryn, I stand at a height of 155 cm.

My songs are available on streaming platforms like Spotify, and I’m constantly learning and improving — not just as a vocalist, but also in songwriting and music production.

I come from a warm and supportive family. I have one older brother, Akif Amzar, and a younger sibling named Wan Alfasyahanim. My biological mother is Hafizah binti Ibrahim, and my first stepmother is Nur Nadirah Hanum binti Mydin. My biological father is Wan Aaqashah bin Mohd Izat Helme, and my stepfather is Mohd Hafis bin Ali. I’m also blessed with two step‑siblings — Rayyan and Rayyna — who bring joy into my everyday life.
